How TrustCar.io Works

Three simple ways to get the information you need before you buy a used car.

1. Start with the right knowledge

Most buyers lose money because they don't know what questions to ask or what to look for. Our digital guides and checklists give you the exact framework used by professionals.

Best for: First-time buyers or anyone who wants to feel prepared before looking at cars.

2. Get data on the specific car

Enter the VIN and receive an instant report with title history, market value in your area, recalls, and ownership records. This is the fastest way to spot major problems or overpriced cars.

Best for: When you're seriously considering a particular vehicle.

3. Get expert eyes on it

For cars over ~$10k or when something in the automated report looks suspicious, have a former dealership manager review everything and give you a written opinion with specific negotiation advice.

Best for: High-stakes purchases where you want a second set of experienced eyes.
