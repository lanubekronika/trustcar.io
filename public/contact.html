Common Questions

Do your reports replace a mechanic inspection?

No. They are an excellent first step and will catch the majority of expensive problems and scams. For expensive or high-mileage vehicles, I still recommend having a trusted independent mechanic do a physical inspection before you buy.

How fast do I get the reports?

Automated reports are delivered instantly by email. Human-reviewed reports are usually completed within 24 hours (often same day).

What if the report doesn't help me?

Every product comes with a 30-day money-back guarantee. If you feel it didn't give you useful information, just email me.

Can I use this for cars I'm selling?

Currently our focus is on buyers. Many sellers do buy the Automated Report to see what buyers will see and to be transparent.
