Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

TrustCar.io ("we", "us") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you use our site and digital products, and how we use it.

Information We Collect

When you purchase a product or request a report, we collect your email address, name (optional), and for VIN reports the VIN you provide. We also collect basic analytics data (page views, referrer) via standard tools to improve the site.

We do not collect credit card information directly — payment processing (when live) is handled by Stripe or similar trusted providers.

How We Use Your Information

  • Deliver your purchased guides or reports via email
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Improve our products and website experience
  • Send occasional product updates (you can unsubscribe anytime)

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share limited data with service providers (email delivery, hosting) solely to operate the business. We comply with applicable privacy laws.

Your Choices

You can request deletion of your data by emailing hello@trustcar.io. Unsubscribe links are included in all emails.

Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. No system is 100% secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be noted with a new "last updated" date. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.

Questions? Contact us at hello@trustcar.io
