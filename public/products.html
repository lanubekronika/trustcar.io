Common Questions

Q: Is this the same as a pre-purchase inspection? ▼

No. This is a remote expert second opinion and data synthesis. We review all available records, local market data, and claims history and deliver clear value and leverage guidance. It is not a physical inspection. Many buyers use our report to decide whether a physical inspection is worth it and exactly what to ask the inspector to focus on.

Q: What if the VIN has no reported history? ▼

A clean history isn't always a good sign — it may mean damage was never reported. Our report flags this and explains what to look for in person and how to negotiate accordingly.

Q: Does this work for cars in all 50 states? ▼

Yes. We pull national title history, accident records, and recalls for any VIN sold in the United States, plus local market comps for your area.
