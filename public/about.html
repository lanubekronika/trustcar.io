Why I built TrustCar.io

For 8 years I worked at a large dealership group. I saw every trick in the book used to move cars and maximize profit at the buyer's expense.

The problem

Most people buying used cars from private sellers have no idea what they're walking into. They see a clean-looking car on Facebook Marketplace, fall in love with the price, and make emotional decisions. By the time problems show up, it's too late.

At the same time, traditional pre-purchase inspections cost $200–$400 and require scheduling a mechanic days in advance. Many buyers skip them entirely.

My approach

I believe buyers deserve better. That's why I created TrustCar.io — to give regular people the same level of information and leverage that dealers use, without the $300 price tag or the need to wait for an appointment.

Every guide and report I create is based on actual situations I've seen hundreds of times. The goal isn't to scare you. It's to give you the facts so you can walk away from bad deals and negotiate confidently on good ones.

What this is not

This is not a replacement for a good mechanic when you're buying a high-mileage or complex vehicle. It's a fast, affordable way to get 80-90% of the protection most buyers need for a fraction of the cost and time.

My promise: Every product I sell is something I would have wanted when I was buying cars for myself or my family. If it doesn't deliver real value, I don't sell it.
