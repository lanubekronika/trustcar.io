IN HIS OWN WORDS

I didn't start behind a desk. I started under cars.

As a technician, I learned how vehicles actually fail — not theory, but real patterns. Which engines burn oil by 60,000 miles. Which transmissions are already failing at 80k. Which repairs on a history report are early signs of something far more expensive.

When I moved into service advisory and management, I saw the other side of the counter. How reconditioning gets rushed before a car hits the lot. How condition gets described versus what we actually found on the lift. How a clean-looking car can have a file full of problems nobody disclosed.

I have worked professionally in the extended warranty claims industry. I reviewed mechanical claims. I studied 3rd-party inspection reports — in-person and virtual — and made the call: approved or denied. I saw what breaks, when, and why. I know what inspectors look for and what the report doesn't always catch.

Most used car buyers never get access to this layer of knowledge. They get a vehicle history PDF and a seller's word.

That's not enough. That's why I built TrustCar.io.
