Why Manny T built TrustCar.io

Manny T
Photo
Manny T — Former Dealership Manager, Tampa, Florida

For over a decade, Manny T worked in dealership service departments and reviewed extended warranties. Manny T saw thousands of deals — the good, the bad, and the outright predatory.

Manny T watched buyers walk in excited and walk out overpaying by thousands or worse, stuck with a car that was a money pit. Manny T saw the tricks sellers use on Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist. Manny T saw how title issues, odometer rollbacks, and flood damage get buried.

Most buyers don't have a chance. They don't know what questions to ask. They don't know what data to pull. They trust their gut or the seller's word.

That's why Manny T built TrustCar.io. To give regular people the same leverage and knowledge that insiders have — without the $300 mechanic bill or the days of waiting.

What this is

Practical, no-hype tools based on real experience. Guides that teach you the red flags. Reports that surface the facts. Analysis that tells you what to do next.

What this isn't

It's not a replacement for a good mechanic on a high-value or complex car. It's not magic. It's information — the kind that used to only be available to people in the business.

Why trust TrustCar?

Because we've been on the other side of the table.

We know how dealers mark up certified pre-owned cars that barely passed inspection. We know how private sellers flip flood cars with fresh carpet and an air freshener. We know what a washed title looks like and how easy it is to miss.

TrustCar exists because the information that protects buyers has always existed — it just wasn't available to buyers. We changed that.

Every tool on this site was built from real experience, not research. That's the difference.
