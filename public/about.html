Why I built TrustCar.io

For over a decade, I worked in dealership service departments and reviewed extended warranties. I saw thousands of deals — the good, the bad, and the outright predatory.

I watched buyers walk in excited and walk out overpaying by thousands or worse, stuck with a car that was a money pit. I saw the tricks sellers use on Facebook Marketplace and Craigslist. I saw how title issues, odometer rollbacks, and flood damage get buried.

Most buyers don't have a chance. They don't know what questions to ask. They don't know what data to pull. They trust their gut or the seller's word.

That's why I built TrustCar.io. To give regular people the same leverage and knowledge that insiders have — without the $300 mechanic bill or the days of waiting.

What this is

Practical, no-hype tools based on real experience. Guides that teach you the red flags. Reports that surface the facts. Analysis that tells you what to do next.

What this isn't

It's not a replacement for a good mechanic on a high-value or complex car. It's not magic. It's information — the kind that used to only be available to people in the business.

My promise: Every product I create is something I would use myself when buying a car for my family. If it doesn't deliver real, actionable value, I won't sell it.

— The founder, former dealership service & warranty manager
